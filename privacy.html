Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 March 2026  ·  Edwin Shibu trading as VerrixAI  ·  Australia

VerrixAI is built around a simple principle: your documents are yours. We do not store, retain, or share any document you upload or paste. Documents are processed in memory and deleted immediately after analysis.

1. Who We Are

VerrixAI is operated by Edwin Shibu, an individual trading as VerrixAI (ABN registered), based in Australia. References to "we", "us", or "our" throughout this policy refer to Edwin Shibu trading as VerrixAI.

Contact us at: [email protected]

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your email address and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Usage data — the number of scans you have performed (a count only — not the content).
  • Payment information — if you subscribe to a paid plan, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not see or store your card details.
  • Communications — if you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence to respond to you.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not store any document you upload or paste into VerrixAI.
  • We do not retain any analysis output generated from your documents.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.
  • We do not use your documents to train AI models.

4. How Your Documents Are Processed

When you submit a document, it is sent securely over HTTPS to our server, forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis, and the result is returned to your browser. Document content is never written to a database or log — it exists in memory only for the duration of the API call.

Anthropic does not store or train on data submitted via their API. They process data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the VerrixAI service.
  • To manage your account and track your scan usage.
  • To process payments for paid plans via Stripe.
  • To send transactional emails such as account confirmations.
  • To respond to support enquiries.

6. Data Storage & Security

Account data (email, scan count) is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS and encrypted at rest. We apply row-level security policies so your account data is accessible only to you.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate VerrixAI:

  • Anthropic — AI document analysis
  • Supabase — authentication and account data storage
  • Stripe — payment processing for paid plans
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — website hosting. Documents pass through Vercel's infrastructure momentarily during processing. Vercel does not retain request body content in logs.

8. Cookies

VerrixAI uses only essential cookies required for authentication (to keep you logged in). We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

9. Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act 1988)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of the personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

Account data (email address and scan count) is deleted within 30 days of account closure upon request.

10. Children

VerrixAI is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email of any material changes. Continued use of VerrixAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at admin@verrixai.com.
